# 📊 Sales Analysis and Forecasting

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales data to identify patterns, trends, and key business insights. It also includes a basic forecasting model to predict future sales based on historical data.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Understand sales trends over time
* Identify high-performing categories, regions, and products
* Perform basic sales forecasting using machine learning

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Scikit-learn

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains information on sales transactions including:

* Order Date
* Sales
* Category and Sub-Category
* Region and State
* Product Name

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning

* Removed irrelevant columns (Row ID, Order ID, Customer ID, etc.)
* Converted date column to datetime format
* Created new features such as Year and Month

---

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### 🔹 Key Analyses Performed

* Total Sales Overview
* Yearly Sales Trend
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Sales by Category
* Regional Sales Analysis
* Top Products Analysis

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Sales show an overall increasing trend over time
* Technology category generates the highest revenue
* West region contributes the most to sales
* A small number of products drive a significant portion of revenue
* Sales peak towards the end of the year, indicating seasonal demand

---

## 🔮 Sales Forecasting

A simple Linear Regression model was used to predict future sales based on yearly trends.

* Model trained using historical yearly sales
* Predicted sales for future years
* Visualization compares actual vs predicted sales

---

## 📈 Results

The forecasting model indicates a continuation of the upward sales trend, suggesting potential growth in future sales.

---
